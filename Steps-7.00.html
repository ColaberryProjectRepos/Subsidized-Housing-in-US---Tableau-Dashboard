Download Tableau Public and Connect to Dataset 7

Download Tableau Public  and Install it.

Click to download Tableau Public   - Enter your email address and download the app. Then Install. 


Download the Dataset 

Click the red icon on top to download Dataset.


Connect to Data source

1. On the Tableau Connect Pane -  Click Microsoft Excel

2. Browse to your downloaded dataset and click on the Excel file

3. Click Open


1. On the Region column - Click Icon 'Abc'              3. Select Country/Region.   

2. Hover on Geographic Role                                4. Select Sheet 1
